## Conclusion

Current verdict: shadow assassin is promising but not passed.

What works:

- Hidden/backline-access mechanics are real and produce many hidden events.
- The branch has a different matchup profile from old execute/poison assassin.
- In the same team shell, shadow `7ж•Ҹ3жӯҰ` performs better against higher waterline buckets than the old default version, despite lower total score.
- `10жӯҰ` can make the branch strong, so physical payoff exists.

What does not work yet:

- No-attribute baseline is weak.
- `10ж•Ҹ` is too weak relative to `10жӯҰ` and `10еқҡйҹ§`.
- In 4v4 route testing, `10еқҡйҹ§` is still the best route and intended output routes have poor focal survival.
- Hidden is not yet a full substitute for survival investment.

## Recommended Next Step

Do not buff assassin HP.

Next design should add reset / exit payoff to the shadow branch:

- If assassin kills during hidden window, refresh a short hidden duration.
- Or consume max mark stacks to re-enter hidden briefly.
- Or reset part of `shadowBurstAmbush` cooldown after a kill / mark detonation.

Goal for the next pass:

- `10ж•Ҹ`, `7ж•Ҹ3жӯҰ`, or `5ж•Ҹ5жӯҰ` should be within about 5 percentage points of the best route in 4v4.
- `10еқҡйҹ§` can remain viable, but should not be clearly best.
- Waterline should keep shadow assassin as a different profile, not simply a stronger old assassin.
